Dal Makhani

90 mins

Main ingredients

  • Black lentils (urad dal)
  • Red kidney beans (rajma)
  • Onion, chopped
  • Tomato, chopped
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Butter or cream
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

    • Instructions

    1. Soak black lentils and kidney beans overnight. Rinse and drain.
    2. Cook the soaked lentils and beans with water until soft.
    3. In a separate pan, saut√© chopped onions until golden.
    4. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
    5. Blend the mixture into a smooth paste.
    6. Combine the cooked lentils, blended paste, red chili powder, and garam masala.
    7. Simmer for a while, then stir in butter or cream.
    8. Garnish with cilantro leaves before serving.
