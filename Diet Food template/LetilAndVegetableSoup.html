Lentil and Vegetable Soup

45 mins

Main ingredients

  • 1 cup dried green or brown lentils, rinsed
  • 6 cups vegetable broth
  • Mixed vegetables (carrots, celery, zucchini, etc.), chopped
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Herbs and spices (thyme, rosemary, cumin, etc.)
  • Salt and pepper

    • Instructions

    1. In a large pot, sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
    2. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
    3. Add dried lentils, vegetable broth, and herbs/spices.
    4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until lentils are tender.
    5. Season with salt and pepper.
