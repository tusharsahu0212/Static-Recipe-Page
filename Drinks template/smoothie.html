Strawberry Banana Smoothie

5 mins

Main ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1 ripe banana, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Ice cubes

    • Instructions

    1. Place strawberries, banana, Greek yogurt, and milk in a blender.
    2. Blend until smooth.
    3. If desired, sweeten with honey or maple syrup.
    4. Add ice cubes and blend again until well combined.
