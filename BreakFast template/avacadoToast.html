Avocado Toast

10 mins

Main ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: poached egg, sliced tomatoes, red pepper flakes

    • Instructions

    1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crisp.
    2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
    3. Mash the avocado with a fork and season with salt and pepper.
    4. Spread the mashed avocado onto the toasted bread slices.
    5. Add your desired toppings, such as a poached egg, sliced tomatoes, or red pepper flakes.
