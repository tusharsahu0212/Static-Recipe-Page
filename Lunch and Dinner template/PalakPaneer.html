Palak Paneer

40 mins

Main ingredients

  • 1 lb paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 bunch spinach, washed and blanched
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • Ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

    • Instructions

    1. Blend blanched spinach into a smooth puree.
    2. Heat ghee or oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
    4. Add garam masala, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and red chili powder. Cook briefly.
    5. Add spinach puree and cook for a few minutes.
    6. Stir in cubed paneer and heavy cream.
    7. Adjust seasoning with salt.
    8. Simmer until the flavors are well combined.
    9. Serve with naan or rice.
