Vegetable Biryani

60 mins

Main ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice, soaked and drained
  • Assorted vegetables (carrots, peas, potatoes, etc.)
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom pods)
  • Biryani masala
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk (optional)
  • Ghee or oil
  • Fresh mint and cilantro leaves for garnish

    • Instructions

    1. Heat ghee or oil in a pot and sauté whole spices until fragrant.
    2. Add thinly sliced onions and cook until caramelized.
    3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
    4. Add assorted vegetables and biryani masala. Cook for a few minutes.
    5. Layer soaked and drained rice over the vegetables.
    6. Pour enough water over the rice, so it's about 1 inch above the rice level.
    7. Add saffron milk (for color) and salt.
    8. Cover and cook until the rice is done and the water is absorbed.
    9. Gently fluff the rice and vegetables.
    10. Garnish with fresh mint and cilantro leaves.
