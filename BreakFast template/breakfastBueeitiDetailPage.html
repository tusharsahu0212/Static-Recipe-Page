Breakfast Burrito

15 mins

Main ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1/4 cup cooked bacon or sausage
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • Salsa or hot sauce (optional)

    • Instructions

    1. Scramble the eggs in a pan, seasoning with salt and pepper.
    2. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
    3. Place scrambled eggs, bacon or sausage, and cheese in the center of each tortilla.
    4. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling, then roll it up.
    5. Serve with salsa or hot sauce if desired.
