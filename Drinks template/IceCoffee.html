Iced Coffee

5 mins

Main ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee, chilled
  • Ice cubes
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Sweetener (sugar, syrup, etc.)

    • Instructions

    1. Brew coffee and let it cool to room temperature, then refrigerate until cold.
    2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
    3. Pour the chilled coffee over the ice.
    4. Add milk or cream and sweetener to taste, if desired.
    5. Stir well and enjoy!
