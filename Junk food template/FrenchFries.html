Classic French Fries

30 mins

Main ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt

    • Instructions

    1. Peel and cut potatoes into fries.
    2. Rinse the cut potatoes to remove excess starch and pat them dry.
    3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or deep skillet.
    4. Fry the potatoes in batches until golden and crispy.
    5. Remove and drain on paper towels, then season with salt.
