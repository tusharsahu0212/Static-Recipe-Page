Classic Apple Crisp

60 mins

Main ingredients

  • 4-5 medium apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • Vanilla ice cream (optional)

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. Toss sliced apples with lemon juice, granulated sugar, and cinnamon. Place in a baking dish.
    3. In a bowl, mix flour, oats, brown sugar, and softened butter to form the topping.
    4. Sprinkle the topping evenly over the apples.
    5. Bake for 40-45 minutes or until the topping is golden and the apples are tender.
    6. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream if desired.
