Homemade Cheeseburger

20 mins

Main ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion (optional toppings)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

    • Instructions

    1. Divide ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
    2. Grill or pan-fry the patties until cooked to your preference.
    3. Place cheese slices on the patties and let them melt.
    4. Toast burger buns.
    5. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments.
