Quinoa and Vegetable Stir-Fry

30 mins

Main ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Low-sodium soy sauce
  • Garlic powder and ginger powder (optional)
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

    • Instructions

    1. Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
    2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
    3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
    4. Season with low-sodium soy sauce, and garlic and ginger powder if using.
    5. Serve vegetables over cooked quinoa.
    6. Sprinkle with sesame seeds for extra flavor and texture./li>
