Veggie Omelette

15 mins

Main ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil

    • Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
    2. Heat butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
    3. Add diced veggies and saut√© until softened.
    4. Pour beaten eggs over the veggies and cook until the edges set.
    5. Sprinkle shredded cheese on one half of the omelette.
    6. Carefully fold the other half over the cheese.
    7. Cook for a minute more until the cheese melts.
    8. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve.
