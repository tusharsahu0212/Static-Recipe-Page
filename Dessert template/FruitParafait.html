Fruit Parfait

10 mins

Main ingredients

  • Greek yogurt
  • Mixed fresh fruits (berries, kiwi, mango, etc.)
  • Granola
  • Honey or maple syrup (optional)

    • Instructions

    1. In serving glasses or bowls, layer Greek yogurt, mixed fruits, and granola.
    2. Repeat the layers until you've used all the ingredients.
    3. Drizzle honey or maple syrup over the top if desired.
