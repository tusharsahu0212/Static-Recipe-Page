Minty Mojito Mocktail

5 mins

Main ingredients

  • 8-10 fresh mint leaves
  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 teaspoons sugar or simple syrup
  • Soda water
  • Crushed ice

    • Instructions

    1. In a glass, muddle mint leaves, lime wedges, and sugar.
    2. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
    3. Top with soda water.
    4. Stir gently and garnish with a mint sprig.
